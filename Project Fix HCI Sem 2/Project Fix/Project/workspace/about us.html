About Our Restaurant

And bam is a provider of brewed tea drinks from Korea that presents several variants of drinks and also several menu variants. In Indonesia, Dan Bam is one of the business units under the umbrella of 3 Idiots Group. In 2002, Dan Bam opened the first store which was in EVERPLATE KEMANG under the auspices of the 3 Idiots group. BINTARO Until this year 3 Idiots Group is planning to open its largest Dan Bam store located in Melawai, South Jakarta.

Serving Process

In the process of serving drinks, Dan Bam uses selected ingredients that are processed using the latest brewing machine. Adopting a customized drink serving concept, Dan Bam's customers can decide for themselves the type of topping and the amount of sugar and ice they want. And Bam also always appears by presenting the latest menu.
